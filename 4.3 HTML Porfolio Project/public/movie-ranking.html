The Best Movies According to Joyce

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fury

An army tank becomes the final hope of salvation against the marauding nazis.
Stars : Shia laBeouf, Brad Pitt.

1917

This movie is based on a real life story of two young british soldiers who are on a mission to deliver a message.

Prisoners

A detective searching for clues and a father desperate to find his kidnapped kids join hands for a common cause.
Stars: Jake Gyllenhal.
